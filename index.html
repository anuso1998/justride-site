About JUSTRIDE

JUSTRIDE LLC is a trusted provider of Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) services in the Kansas City area and beyond. We are dedicated to bridging transportation gaps, ensuring that every member of our community has safe, dependable rides to medical appointments, care facilities, and essential services.

Who We Are

Our team is built on trust, respect, and a commitment to deliver high-quality transportation with professionalism and care.

Our Mission

We believe transportation should never be a barrier to receiving care. Our mission is to ensure that every ride is safe, timely, and dignified.

How We Do It

With trained drivers, a reliable fleet, and dedicated support staff, JUSTRIDE makes transportation worry-free for our members and healthcare partners.
